This website compares various Science Fiction movies/shows.

We will compare the following movies:

  • Star Wars
  • Star Trek
  • Back to the Future

Universes

Star Wars takes place a long, long time ago in a galaxy far, far away. There are many, many planetary systems, and most of the time they are one union, whether the Galactic Republic or Galactic Empire. Most planets have different types of aliens.

Back to the Future takes place in the not-so-distant past on Earth. It does, however, contain time travelling, and it does have alternate timelines.

Star Trek takes place in the future in our galaxy. There are also different planets, which also contain alien species.

Plots

Star Wars has more of a storyline throughout the movies or series. In the first triad, episodes I, II, and III, there is an evil Sith Lord masterminding both sides of a war in order to gain all the power. In episodes IV, V, and VI, the heroes fight to end the rule of the evil Emperor Palpatine. In epsiodes VII, VIII, and IX, the heroes try to end the oppression of the First Order.

Back to the Future has a storyline as well; in the first movie Marty tries to save Doc from being killed, in the second, Doc takes Marty to the future, and Biff makes trouble for them, and in Back to the Future 3, Marty saves Doc from being stuck in 1885.

Star Trek has different issues specific to each episode, usually a run-in with a new species or an unidentified distress call.

Vehicles

In Back to the Future there are few different ways of getting around, the main ones being the Delorean and skateboards/hoverboards.

In Star Wars, there are many, many types of spaceships, a notable one being the Millennium Falcon.

The Enterprise, in both the original Star Trek and The Next Generation , is the major means of conveyance.

Weapons

In Back to the Future they have standard guns.

Not only are the characters different; they also have different weapons.
