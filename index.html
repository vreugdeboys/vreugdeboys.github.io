This website compares various Science Fiction movies/shows.

Star Wars

Perhaps the most well known of science fiction universes. It has multiple spin-offs, including Rebels, The Mandalorian, The Clone Wars, Freemaker (Lego), and Rogue One.

It was created in 1977 by George Lucas.

It involves certain individuals who can use a mystical energy field called The Force. The users of the light side of the Force are called Jedi, and the users of the dark side are called Sith.

Star Trek

There is a great divide separating Star Trek and Star Wars fans.

Star Trek was actually created more than 10 years before Star Wars, in 1966 by Gene Roddenberry.

It also has many spin-offs, including The Next Generation, Deep Space Nine, Enterprise, and Voyager.

Back to the Future

Back to the Future was created in 1985.

It involves a time-traveling car called "the Delorean". When it is driven at 88 miles per hour, it time travels.

There are three parts. First they time travel to 1955, then to 2015, then 1885.
