This website compares various Science Fiction movies/shows.

We will compare the following movies:

  • Star Wars
  • Star Trek
  • Back to the Future

Characters

Universes

Star Wars takes place a long, long time ago in a galaxy far, far away.
Back to the Future takes place in the not-so-distant past on Earth.
Star Trek takes place in the future in our galaxy.

Plots

Star Wars has more of a storyline,
Back to the Future has a storyline as well,
but Star Trek has different issues specific to each episode.

Vehicles

In Back to the Future there are few different ways of getting around, the main ones being the Delorean and skateboards/hoverboards.

In Star Wars, there are many, many types of spaceships, a notable one being the Millennium Falcon.

The Enterprise, in both the original Star Trek and The Next Generation, is the major means of conveyance.

Weapons

Not only are the characters different; they also have different weapons.
