This website compares various Science Fiction movies/shows.

Star Wars

Perhaps the most well known of science fiction universes.

It has multiple spin-offs, including Rebels, The Mandalorian, The Clone Wars, Freemaker (Lego), and Rogue One.

It was created in 1977 by George Lucas.

It involves certain individuals who can use a mystical energy field called The Force.

The users of the light side of the Force are called Jedi, and the users of the dark side are called Sith.
